Privacy Policy

IB DT 2027 — Teacher Dashboard
Last updated: March 2026
No Data Collected

Overview

IB DT 2027 is a standalone classroom management tool for IB Design Technology teachers. It is designed with privacy as a core principle. The app does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers.

Data Storage

All information you enter into the app — including student names, scores, reports, photos, and assessment data — is stored exclusively on your device using local browser storage (localStorage).

No data is sent to the developer, to third-party services, or to any remote server.

iCloud Sync

When you choose to use iCloud sync, your data is transferred to your personal iCloud account using Apple's built-in iCloud Documents framework. This data is stored in your own iCloud Drive container and is governed by Apple's privacy policy.

The developer has no access to your iCloud data. Sync is initiated manually by the user and can be disabled at any time.

File Access

The app allows you to link student work folders via the iOS Files picker. Access to these folders is granted by you through Apple's standard document picker and is limited to the folders you explicitly select. The app does not access any other files on your device.

What We Do Not Do

  • We do not collect analytics or usage data
  • We do not use tracking cookies or advertising identifiers
  • We do not integrate any third-party SDKs, frameworks, or services
  • We do not require account creation or sign-in
  • We do not make any network requests
  • We do not access your camera, microphone, contacts, or location

Student Data

This app is designed for use by teachers to manage their own classroom records. Any student information entered is under the sole control of the teacher using the app. The developer never has access to student data.

Teachers are responsible for handling student data in accordance with their school's data protection policies and applicable regulations (e.g. GDPR, FERPA).

Data Export

You can export all your data at any time using the built-in JSON export feature. You can also delete all data at any time using the "Clear All Data" function within the app.

Children's Privacy

This app is designed for use by teachers, not students. It does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. Student names and scores entered by teachers are stored locally on the teacher's device only.

Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
